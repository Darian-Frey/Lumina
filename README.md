## What is Lumina?

Lumina is an **interactive simulation suite** for physics and mathematics, targeting the gap that has existed too long between two extremes:

- **Below** ‚ÄĒ Free tools like PhET are excellent but stop at introductory level
- **Above** ‚ÄĒ MATLAB, Mathematica, and COMSOL cost hundreds or thousands of pounds and require institutional licences

Lumina lives in between. It is designed for the student who has outgrown classroom simulations but cannot afford enterprise software ‚ÄĒ covering everything from A-level fundamentals to graduate-level research topics.

## Highlights

**RotoCurve** ‚úÖ ‚ÄĒ Fit MOND, CODA, and Newtonian dark matter models to real SPARC galaxy rotation curve data. The only tool of its kind outside of bespoke research scripts.

**Chaotic Double Pendulum** ‚ÄĒ Real-time trajectory tracing with Lyapunov exponent readout.

**Fourier Synthesiser** ‚ÄĒ Build any waveform from harmonics and watch the series converge live.

**Quantum Tunnelling** ‚ÄĒ Vary barrier width and height, watch transmission probability update instantly.

**Ising Model** ‚ÄĒ 2D ferromagnet with Metropolis Monte Carlo, visualise the phase transition as temperature sweeps through T_c.

**FractalLab** ‚ÄĒ Mandelbrot and Julia sets with deep zoom and custom iteration rules.

**Tiling Lab** ‚ÄĒ Penrose and Ammann-Beenker aperiodic tilings with local isomorphism explorer.

**ODE Phase Portrait** ‚ÄĒ Nullclines, trajectories, fixed points, and stability classification for any 2D system.

## Roadmap

### Phase 1 ‚ÄĒ Launch Set (v0.1)
Ten simulations targeting broad appeal and low build effort. Designed to ship fast and validate the market.

`M04` Simple Harmonic Motion ¬∑ `M10` Chaotic Double Pendulum ¬∑ `W01` Wave Superposition ¬∑ `W02` Fourier Synthesiser ¬∑ `T01` Ideal Gas ¬∑ `T02` Maxwell-Boltzmann ¬∑ `AP01` ODE Phase Portrait ¬∑ `AP02` Bifurcation Diagram ¬∑ `AP03` Lorenz Attractor ¬∑ `P03` FractalLab

### Phase 2 ‚ÄĒ Core Expansion (v0.2‚Äďv0.3)
Quantum, astrophysics, and electromagnetism pillars. Introduces RotoCurve as the first research-grade module.

### Phase 3 ‚ÄĒ University Tier (v0.4‚Äďv0.5)
Medium-effort modules for first and second year undergraduates.

### Phase 4 ‚ÄĒ Research & Special Topics (v1.0)
High-effort and graduate-level modules. Premium tier.

Full details in [`SIMS_SPEC.md`](SIMS_SPEC.md).
